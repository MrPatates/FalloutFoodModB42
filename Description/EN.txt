[h1]This mod is a port of the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2843941815] Fallout Food mod [/url] to B42.[/h1]

[h2]This mod adds in pre-war food from the Fallout universe[/h2]

When you open a Nuka Cola you will receive a bottle cap, which could be useful for trading purposes.
Items can be found both in shops and houses.

[h2] Items include : [/h2]

[h3]Drinks : [/h3]
[list]
[*]Nuka Cola,
[*]Sunset Sarsaparilla,
[/list]
[h3]Food : [/h3]
[list]
[*]SugarBombs
[*]Cram
[*]InstaMash
[*]PorknBeans
[*]Fancy Lads Snack Cakes
[*]BlamCo Mac & Cheese
[*]Big Pops Bubblegum
[*]Dandy Boy Apples
[*]Yum Yum Deviled Eggs
[*]Cotton Candy Bites
[/list]
